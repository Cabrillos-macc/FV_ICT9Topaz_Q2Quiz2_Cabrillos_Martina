Inside Out 2 Characters

Emotions Images
☆Joy☆

Joy gives you the childhood you imagine-Joy! She valued what Riley, main character, would feel at every important event in her life. In fact, she forgot every single other emotion trying to value the perfection of what was going on inside Riley. Not seeing that perfection is letting Riley feel what is meant to be felt.
☆Anger☆

Times would come where things, events, situations, and even people would cause you to yell or show your unique act of "anger". This would be due to your little red emotion-Anger-taking control on the board in the tower. He is always mad and would always be controlled with other emotions. However, Anger would fire up when the situation is fueled.
☆Sadness☆

Have you ever cried over the smallest or biggest things? Well, maybe Sadness touched the controls without the sight of Joy. Even if Sadness causes a not so pleasing reaction, she serves importance. Like life, balance is needed. Same goes for our emotions. Having the urge to cry or frown is needed as it lets us release what is needed to be released.
